Low-Cost Earthquake Detection Alarm System (LCEDAS)

The LCEDAS is a cost-effective earthquake detection and alarm system using Arduino technology and accelerometer sensors. It was designed to enhance community preparedness in earthquake-prone areas of the Philippines.

The project demonstrates high functionality and efficiency, showing my passion for innovation and safety technologies.
